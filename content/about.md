# About The Libre Life

The Libre Life was created with a single goal: to prove that reclaiming your digital privacy and switching to open-source software doesn't have to be a frustrating, broken experience.

On modern hardware, Linux is faster, safer, and more powerful than ever. This site serves as the central hub for all my video tutorials, script repositories, and written guides. Whether you are trying to fix a Wi-Fi lag spike in your favorite game, set up a secure local server, or simply ditch proprietary software for good, you will find the step-by-step documentation here.

The Setup:
All content for The Libre Life is proudly produced on open-source software. The videos are recorded on a ThinkPad running openSUSE Tumbleweed, edited natively in Kdenlive, and this website is statically generated for maximum speed and security.
